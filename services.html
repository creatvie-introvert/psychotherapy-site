Therapy Services

Everyone's emotional landscape is unique. I offer a safe, collaborative space to explore what's going on beneath the surface, with the goal of creating lasting emotional insight and change.

Therapy Services Offered

Short-Term
Therapy

This approach is focused and time-limited, typically lasting between 6 and 12 sessions. It's helpful if you're working through a specific issue, such as a recent life event, a relationship difficulty, or a sudden emotional challenge. Together, we'll explore the immediate concern and develop strategies for understanding and coping.

Long-Term
Therapy

Long-term therapy offers the space to explore deeper emotional patterns that may have developed over time. This might include ongoing struggles with self-esteem, long-standing relationship dynamics, or unresolved past experiences. The work unfolds gradually and supports meaningful, lasting emotional insight and growth.

Open-Ended
Therapy

Open-ended therapy provides a flexible, ongoing opportunity for personal exploration and self-understanding. There's no set time limit—therapy continues for as long as it feels useful to you. This format is especially supportive if you're navigating complex or evolving emotional experiences at your own pace.

Areas I Can Help With

  • - Anxiety and stress
  • - Depression and low mood
  • - Relationship difficulties
  • - Attachment and trust issues
  • - Identity and self-worth
  • - Grief and loss
  • - Childhood experiences and trauma
  • - Life transitions and emotional overwhelm

Fees & Sessions

Sessions last 50 minutes and are held remotely via telephone or video, providing a safe and confidential space from the comfort of your own home.

The fee is £70 per session, with a reduced rate of £45 for the initial consultation. Weekly appointments are arranged at a regular time that works for you.

If you have any questions about fees or availability, please don't hesitate to get in touch.
