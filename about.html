About Me

A warm, grounded space for emotional healing and self-discovery

Therapist Bio

I'm a warm, experienced psychodynamic counsellor with a background in social work and mental health. My therapeutic approach is grounded in compassion, curiosity, and creating a safe, non-judgemental space where you can explore your inner world.

With over 10 years of clinical counselling experience and more than 30 years working in social care, I bring deep insight into the complexities of human emotion, identity, and relationships. I work with adults from a wide range of backgrounds, and have particular experience supporting individuals facing anxiety, loss, low self-esteem, and identity-related concerns.

My practice is guided by a strong belief in the healing potential of therapy and the importance of working at a pace that feels right for each individual. I aim to be a steady and compassionate presence — someone who helps you feel safe enough to look inward and grow.
