Emergency Contacts & Support Services

Here you'll find practical support resources, including emergency contact information and useful links for mental health and wellbeing.

Emergency Services

Call 999 — For immediate danger or life-threatening situations.

NHS 111

Call 111 — Non-emergency medical advice and mental health support.

Samaritans

Call 116 123 (Free, 24/7) — Emotional support for anyone in distress.
www.samaritans.org

Shout

Text 'SHOUT' to 85258 (Free, 24/7) — Confidential text support.
www.giveusashout.org

Mind

InfoLine: 0300 123 3393 — Mental health information and support.
www.mind.org.uk

The Mix (under 25s)

Call 0808 808 4994 or text 'THEMIX' to 85258 — Support for young people.
www.themix.org.uk
